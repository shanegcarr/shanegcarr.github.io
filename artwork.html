Radial I

Acrylic on canvas; 2019

❮ ❯

I was inspired by the abstract, simple, and bold use of primary colors in the many pieces by Piet Mondrian, such as his famous Composition II in Red, Blue, and Yellow. I also liked the idea of breaking the piece apart into multiple canvases, leaving the rest of the circles to be filled in by the imagination.

Everyday Company

Acrylic on canvas; 2021

❮ ❯

I was inspired by the pop art of Andy Warhol depicting everyday objects in outlandish colors, such as his famous Campbell's Soup Cans. Smart phones and social media are likely the most frequently viewed objects in our lives. A daily essential, they provide a comforting, yet somewhat ominous, companionship.
